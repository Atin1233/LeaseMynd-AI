# LeaseAI User Guide

## Getting Started

### 1. Create Your Account
Sign up at LeaseAI using your email address. You'll be prompted to create or join an organization.

### 2. Set Up Your Organization
In **Settings**, add your organization name. This enables team collaboration and shared lease management.

### 3. Upload Your First Lease
1. Go to **New Analysis** (or Dashboard → Upload)
2. Drag and drop a PDF lease document, or click to browse
3. Optionally add property address and property type for better market comparisons
4. Click **Upload**

### 4. Run AI Analysis
1. After upload, open the lease from your dashboard
2. Click **Analyze with AI**
3. Wait 2–5 minutes for the analysis to complete
4. Review the risk score, executive summary, and clause-by-clause analysis

### 5. Act on Recommendations
- **Risk Score:** 80–100 = Low risk, 60–79 = Medium, 40–59 = High, 0–39 = Critical
- **Executive Summary:** Top findings in plain English
- **Clause Analysis:** Expand each section for details
- **Generate Improved Lease:** Get AI-drafted revisions with replacement language

---

## Understanding Risk Scores

| Score Range | Risk Level | Meaning |
|-------------|------------|---------|
| 80-100 | Low | Favorable terms, balanced lease |
| 60-79 | Medium | Some concerns, generally acceptable |
| 40-59 | High | Significant issues; negotiate before signing |
| 0-39 | Critical | Major problems; consider walking away |

---

## Supported Files

- **Formats:** PDF (primary), Word documents
- **Max Size:** 50MB
- **Max Pages:** 100
- **OCR:** Supported for scanned documents

---

## Team Collaboration

- **Invite Members:** Settings → Team → Invite by email
- **Roles:** Owner, Admin, Member, Viewer
- **Comments:** Click any clause to add comments; team members can reply
- **Activity Feed:** View recent team activity on the dashboard

---

## Market Comparison

On analyzed leases, scroll to **Market Comparison** to see how your terms compare to regional benchmarks for:

- Rent per square foot
- TI (tenant improvement) allowances
- Concession packages
- Lease term norms

- **Export PDF:** Download full analysis from the lease detail page

## Troubleshooting

**Analysis failed?**
- Ensure the PDF has searchable text (not just images)
- Try re-uploading
- Check file size (under 50MB)

**Slow analysis?**
- Long documents (50+ pages) take 3–5 minutes
- Rate limits apply: 5 analyses per 15 minutes on standard plans

**Need help?**
- Visit **Help Center** in the dashboard
- Contact support via the Contact page
