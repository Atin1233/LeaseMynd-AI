## 2. Company Logo

### Specifications:
- **Size:** 300 x 300 pixels (square)
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **File Size:** Under 8MB
- **Background:** Transparent or solid color (recommend transparent)
- **Content:** Company logo only (no text, taglines, or additional graphics)

### Best Practices:
- Use high-resolution version of `leasemyndaiapplogo.png`
- Ensure logo is centered and clearly visible
- Test how it looks at small sizes (it appears small in feeds)
- Avoid complex details that won't be visible when small
- Ensure good contrast against LinkedIn's white/light backgrounds

### Design Tips:
- Simple, recognizable icon or wordmark
- Professional and modern
- Consistent with website and marketing materials
- Works well in both light and dark modes (if applicable)

## 3. Cover Image (Banner)

### Specifications:
- **Size:** 1192 x 220 pixels (recommended)
- **Format:** PNG or JPG
- **File Size:** Under 8MB
- **Safe Zone:** Keep important content within 1192 x 220 pixels (content may be cropped on mobile)

### Recommended Design Elements:

**Option A - Branded Banner:**
- Company logo (left or center)
- Tagline: "AI-Powered Commercial Lease Analysis"
- Subtagline: "Turn 30 hours of legal review into 5 minutes of AI insight"
- Visual elements: Subtle real estate/technology graphics
- Color scheme: Professional, aligns with brand colors

**Option B - Value Proposition:**
- Main headline: "Reduce Lease Risk by 80%"
- Supporting text: "AI-powered analysis in minutes, not weeks"
- Visual: Clean, modern design with data visualization elements

**Option C - Problem-Solution:**
- Problem statement: "Spending $25K+ annually on lease review?"
- Solution: "LeaseMynd AI analyzes leases in 5 minutes"
- Visual: Professional, trustworthy imagery

### Design Best Practices:
- Keep text minimal and readable
- Use high-contrast colors for text
- Include call-to-action if space allows
- Mobile-responsive (test on mobile view)
- Professional imagery (avoid stock photos that look generic)
- Consistent with brand identity

### Text Guidelines:
- **Maximum 2-3 lines of text**
- **Large, readable font** (at least 24pt equivalent)
- **Clear value proposition**
- **No contact information** (that goes in About section)
